The Monkees

For over 50 years, the Monkees are recognised as the one of the most successful bands of the 60's with international hits such as "Last Train to Clarksville" and "I'm a Believer". Despite the band breaking up in 1971, the band have continued to tour and record new hits. So, we have dedicated this fans site to all the wonderful Monkees followers who want to listen to their classic hits as we all stay on top of their most recent records. The Monkees are also available for private bookings - whether its Wedding / Business / or Seasonal events. The Monkees are currently taking bookings for their most recent album, "The Christmas Party".
